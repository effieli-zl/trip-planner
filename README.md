# Trip Planner

**Plan trips, travel together, tell the story — all in one place.**

Trip Planner is a personal and group travel tool that grows with you from the
first search to the final post. Your itinerary is the single source of truth —
planning, collaboration, journaling, and content generation all build on top of it.

## Why Trip Planner

Existing tools are either planning-only (no sharing, no content layer) or
social-only (no serious planning). Trip Planner does the full loop:

- **Plan** — build a rich, day-by-day itinerary: flights, stays, activities,
  bookings, budgets, and a live map.
- **Travel** — record the trip as it happens, on mobile, even offline.
- **Share** — turn your itinerary and journal into a ready-to-publish story.

## Who it's for

- Individual travelers who plan seriously and want to remember and share the trip.
- Small groups (couples, friends, family) who co-plan and travel together.

## Status

Trip Planner is in early development. We're validating the core planning
experience first as a lightweight AI-assisted tool, then growing into the full
web + mobile product.
