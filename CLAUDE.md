# trip-planner

Personal and group trip planning tool — itinerary, booking, and sharing.
The itinerary is the single source of truth; collaboration, journaling, and
content generation all build on top of it.

**Status:** Greenfield. Planning a mini "AI Skill" version (Phase 0) before the
full product build.
